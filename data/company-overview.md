# Aroma Cloud — Company Overview

Aroma Cloud is a Series A specialty-coffee subscription company founded in 2021
in Seattle, WA. We source single-origin beans from cooperatives in Ethiopia,
Colombia, and Guatemala, roast them in our Tukwila facility, and deliver to
~38,000 monthly subscribers across the US and Canada.

## Mission

Make great coffee accessible — every bag traceable to the farm, every roast
shipped within 72 hours of profile-confirmation, every cup priced fairly for
both grower and drinker.

## Numbers (as of Q4 2025)

- ARR: $14.2M
- Subscribers: 38,400 active, 9.1% monthly churn
- Avg order value: $32.50
- Roasting capacity: 4,200 lbs/week
- Headcount: 47 (28 ops, 12 engineering, 5 GTM, 2 finance)
